== Description ==
  
A plugin to play music in the background when the site is loaded, add a floating button on all pages to pause or resume music.
  
== Installation ==
  
1. Upload the plugin folder to your /wp-content/plugins/ folder.
1. Go to the **Plugins** page and activate the plugin.
  
== Frequently Asked Questions ==
  
= How do I use this plugin? =
  
Step 1) After activating plugin navigae to setting.
Step 2) There is a option calle background music, kindly open it.
Step 3) Feed the uploaded music URL select position of your control button in frontend and click the checkbox to enble it.
Step 4) Click on save button and open your site to check it.
  
= How to uninstall the plugin? =
  
Simply deactivate and delete the plugin. 
  
= Control not showing after saving settings? =

Kindly hard refresh your site or try opening in incognito mode, its mainly due to your browser cache
